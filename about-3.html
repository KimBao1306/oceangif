2015

Ocean Gift Foods established on May 6th, 2015.

2016

In early of 2016, we build our processing plant in Long Hau Industrial Zone. From these it takes 45 minutes to HCM city center and 60 minutes to Cat Lai Terminal, main port of HCM City. It is also 10 minutes from new HCM Terminal names TCHP Port that developing and will share its capacity with Cat Lai Terminal in near future. Our Processing Plant is within 13,980m2 area and included 6 processing line with 8000MT anual capacity.

2017

We had our 1st order exported to Japan in April 2017 and applied our EU code: DL843 to comply EU export. We also achieved ISO, HACCP, BRC, Sedex, MSC, ASC certificate and manage the procesing plant under international standard.

2018

We started to export our product to EU, Australia, Korean and North America Market and it cover 30% of our income from other market excluded Japan. We have maximize our 6 processing line and stable the production.

2019

We expand other 3 processing line to make total 9 processing line in our plant during 2nd haft of 2019. From there it increase our Anual Capacity to 10,000 MT
